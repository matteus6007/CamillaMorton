Manolo Blahník's and the Tale of the Elves and the Shoemaker

Once Again US Vogue launched the series with exclusive interview online. This was followed with articles across US and UK, and a press launch and signing with Manolo Blahnik and Camilla Morton in his New York Store.

Mr Blahnik: "It's divine, it's wonderful, I love the book! It was a very fun adventure. I have never done such a thing before but, yes I enjoyed myself doing it..."
