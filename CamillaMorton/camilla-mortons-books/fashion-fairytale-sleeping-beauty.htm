Christian Lacroix And the Tale of Sleeping Beauty

Illustrated by Christian Lacroix

Can dormant Beauty really be awakened by a Princely touch?

The classic story of Sleeping Beauty has had us believing so for years. Now, spun magically from the threads of this much beloved fairytale, comes the enchanting memoir of celebrated fashion designer Christian Lacroix. Fairy tale and haute couture mix charmingly in this re-imagined story by fashion writer and international bestselling author Camilla Morton, and is illustrated exclusively by the tale's hero - Monsieur Lacroix himself.

Christian Lacroix and the Tale of Sleeping Beauty is new twist on a time-honored tale will cast a spell, revive childhood fantasies and reassure you that dreams really can come true...

About the Fashion Fairytale Memoir Series

Each book in this inspired series from fashion writer Camilla Morton inventively re-imagines one of our favorite "Once Upon a Time" stories, blending with it the real-life story of a famed fashion designer. Featuring lush illustrations drawn by the designers themselves, these tales are a unique collaboration that illuminate each iconic individual's creative magic, while celebrating their unique lives and careers. The result is an intriguing combination of whimsy and memoir.

The series launches with this contemporary twist on the tale of Sleeping Beauty, as Monsieur Lacroix exercises a stunningly diverse array of artistic styles, revealing the many more mesmerizing sides to his fertile imagination.
